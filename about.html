About Hummus Republic

The Mediterranean
Chipotle of America

A fast-casual restaurant serving quick Mediterranean food made with fresh, delicious ingredients. Our name comes from the Arabic word for the hummus bean — the garbanzo — and you'll find it in every station: the base, the hummuses, the falafel and the toppings.
